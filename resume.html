Resume

I am a bioengineer interested in creating low-cost biological solutions to real world problems using the tools of synthetic biology. I believe that enabling people to create their own biotechnologies could help make this world a more sustainable place to live. I am currently a Junior lecturer of Biology at the University of Guanajuato in Mexico.
